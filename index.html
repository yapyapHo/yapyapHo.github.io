Research Interests

My goal is to develop an accurate medical imaging model that can perfectly denoise messy CT scans with CT artifacts that can make it difficult for doctors to diagnose.

I have been focusing in reconstructing CT scans by interpolation and extrapolation using neural networks, and also trying to build up key knowledge in various fields.

Recently I'm working on 4DCT image (CT scan with time axis) interpolation, to make organs' movement in CT scan continuous over time for better visualization and to remove various artifacts.
